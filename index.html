Machine Learning

Built for Scale and Security

Insurance only works if the math checks out. We leverage Python-based AI models on the backend to dynamically protect liquidity and prevent malicious abuse.

  • 1

    Dynamic Risk Pricing (XGBoost)

    Every Sunday, the AI pulls the 7-day weather forecast. If a massive monsoon week is predicted, the risk pool adjusts the weekly premium slightly higher to ensure payouts. Safe-zone workers get discounts.

  • 2

    Fraud Catching (Isolation Forest)

    If a worker attempts to use a "Mock Location" GPS app to spoof their coordinates into a flooded zone, the Isolation Forest model detects the inactivity anomaly and automatically flags the claim.
